The Nodejs + Angular multi-language webapp offers a versatile solution for managing leads and sales of franchise promotion spaces. The application is capable of supporting multiple languages and domains, allowing for seamless global expansion. The use of Nodejs and Angular allows for efficient performance and easy customization, making it a valuable tool for organizations looking to grow their franchise presence. With this webapp, businesses can manage their leads and sales effectively, providing a high-quality experience for their customers.
